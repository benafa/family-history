--- layout: single title: How to contribute? --- If you're wondering how you can contribute, here are a few suggestions:
  • Did you notice anyone missing from the family trees? I would be happy to add them. For people from generations ago, it will be helpful to provide documentation showing their relationships. This leads me to the next suggestion....
  • Do you have any primary source material? This includes documents such as marriage certificates, birth records, obituaries, newspapers, memorial cards, obituaries, and photos. The website would greatly benefit from your contributions.
    • What about secondary sources? Secondary sources can be a great way of finding hints, but their accuracy can come into question. In order to keep the website as accurate as possible, I kindly request that that you refrain from providing information from secondary sources (such as Worldgenweb). Thanks for understanding!
  • Have you seen any mistakes? Firstly, my apologies! Please don't hesitate to bring any errors to my attention.
  • Lastly, please consider spreading the word about Colombo Chetty Genealogy's website, https://colombochetty.com. You will help more people find out about their ancestry and potentially fill in gaps in the website.
Please send any questions or content contributions to ben@colombochetty.com. Thank you for your support and help in expanding the project!

Research Questions - Can you help? 🔍

I have a few questions for you all based on this week's photo of the week...
  • Do you have any photos of Joy Casie Chetty, particularly with his siblings?
  • Do you have any photos of Poomani Fernandopulle, particularly with her siblings?
  • Who are the siblings of Poomani's father Anthony Joseph Fernandopulle?
  • Who are the siblings of Joy's grandfather Casper Casie Chetty?
  • What is the maidan name of Joy's paternal grandmother (i.e. A.P. Casie Chetty's mother)?
If you have any memory of these people, know someone who might, or have relevant primary source material, please let me know!
